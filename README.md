# Anflix Frontend

Static multi-page frontend for Anflix with unique layouts per section.

## Pages
- `index.html` (Home)
- `Pages/movies.html`
- `Pages/series.html`
- `Pages/trailers.html`
- `Pages/upcoming.html`
- `Pages/contact.html`

## Sections On Home
- Explore Sections (Movies, Series, Trailers)
- Upcoming Movies & Series (2 columns x 3 rows)
- Popular Movies
- Trending Movies Series
- Ad panel in the hero area

## Ads Panel
- "Book Now" links to `Pages/contact.html`

## Upcoming Posters
- `images/Scream 7.jpg` used for items 1 and 4
- `images/Cold Storage.jpg` used for items 2 and 5
- `images/Protector.jpg` used for items 3 and 6

## Popular Movies Posters
- Popular Movies uses the same poster images for its six ranked items.

## Trending Movies Posters
- Trending Movies Series uses the same poster images for its items.

## Styling
- Upcoming poster thumbnails use `background-size: contain`, centered and no-repeat to show the full image.

## Welcome Carousel
- Welcome Back section includes a movie carousel with left/right controls and auto-slide.


## Footer Links
- Contact and Advertise link to `Pages/contact.html`
- Privacy links to `privacy.pdf`
